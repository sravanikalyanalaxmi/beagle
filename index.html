The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is breed of small hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling.Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the Beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and fiidstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size,good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
